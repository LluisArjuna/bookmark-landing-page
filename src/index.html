A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync across all your devices, so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in One Click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent Search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time. No need to scroll through all your bookmarks.

Share Your Bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send with the click of a button.
